## ✅ FROZEN SERVICE #1 — Wedding Venue

### Demand side (how the user books)
1. User creates a **Wedding** (parent object): couple names, date/muhurat, city, guest count, budget — auto-saved as **draft**, resume anytime. Date + city + guest count flow down to every service as pre-applied filters.
2. From the Wedding → open **Venue**.
3. Entry: **search by venue name** OR **enter location / detect current location** → venue list.
4. **Filters** (date availability pre-applied): capacity vs guest count, rent/budget, type (hall/lawn/farmhouse/hotel/resort), amenities (AC, parking, rooms, power backup), vendor policy (outside caterer allowed?).
5. Each card shows an **availability badge for the date** — ✅ Free / ⚠️ Few slots / ❌ Booked.
6. Venue detail: photos, peak/off-peak pricing, capacity, rules/conditions, reviews, availability calendar, **request site visit / virtual tour**.
7. **Pay token → date fixed** → digital agreement (token via escrow). Spectrum supported: _Enquire → Site visit → Token → Confirm._

### Supply side (venue owners)
- Self-register & list: photos, capacity, peak/off-peak pricing, amenities, **conditions** (mandatory: no outside caterer, décor-by-us, timing/alcohol rules; plus optional).
- **Owner dashboard:** manage listings, block/open dates on a calendar, accept/decline requests.
- **Verified before going live** (real owner, real photos, license/compliance).

### Venue listing — config fields
Capacity (seated & **comfortable/floating**) · venue type · peak/off-peak rent · **all-inclusive price breakdown** (rent + taxes + service charge + generator + valet) · **overtime rate/hr** · **power-backup scope** (lights-only / partial / full AC + KVA) · amenities (AC, parking capacity, guest rooms) · **inclusions checklist** (hours window, setup, cleanup, chairs/tables/AV) · **vendor policy** (outside caterer allowed? penalty?) · décor/music/alcohol rules · verified photos · reviews · availability calendar · location/map · **license/compliance badge**.

_Anti-bypass / revenue model: deferred (notes for later — mask owner contact, route payments & comms through platform, anti-circumvention clause, on-platform-only value)._

## ✅ FROZEN SERVICE #2 — Catering

**Model:** one catering order **per function**; user can pick a **different caterer per function** or reuse one across functions.

### Per-function catering order
- **Meal type:** breakfast / lunch / hi-tea / dinner / snacks
- **Menu:** course-structured builder — welcome drinks → starters → mains (breads/rice/dal/sabzi) → live counters → sweets/dessert → paan; "pick N per course"; **presets + custom**; **community menu templates** (e.g., traditional Maharashtrian thali)
- **Diet/religious profile:** pure-veg-only / Jain / satvik (no onion-garlic) / non-veg / halal
- **Headcount:** pulled from the function's guest count + **smart buffer** (realistic attendance); final-count cutoff
- **Service:** style (pangat / buffet / plated) + **staff ratio** (waiters per 100) + serviceware tier
- **Special meals:** kids, elderly/diabetic (sugar-free), satvik for rituals
- **Add-ons:** live counters (priced per head), welcome drinks & water, sweets/halwai & dessert/cake, paan, bar (if venue allows)

### Caterer onboarding (supply side — self-serve)
Profile · cuisines & specialties · sample menus + per-plate tiers · real food/plating photos · **FSSAI + hygiene + insurance** · capacity (max events/plates per day) · **date-wise availability** · service styles · staff & equipment · service cities · reviews.

### Signature features (differentiators)
- **All-in transparent per-plate** + line-item breakdown (no 25–40% hidden inflation)
- **"No shortage, no waste"** smart headcount + buffer calculator
- **Leftover-donation** tie-up (NGO) — CSR + marketing
- **On-site freshness guarantee**
- **Tasting booking** before confirmation
- **Auto venue-compatibility check** (kitchen / power / space + vendor policy)

### Process
Tasting logistics (who / when / how many) · menu-lock date · change/cancellation policy · **final billing on actual headcount** (per-plate × confirmed count; buffer billing rules).

## ✅ FROZEN SERVICE #3 — Photography (Photo + Video, bundled)

**Model:** one **bundled photo + video** booking (not separate photo/video vendors). Coverage spans **Functions** (which events to cover) + optional **pre-wedding shoot**. Supply ranges from **solo photographers → full studios**.

### Packages & deliverables (all-in, transparent)
- Coverage: which functions · hours per function · **team size** per function · photo + cinematography
- Deliverables itemized: # edited photos · album (size/pages/material, included?) · film length · teaser/reels · drone · **RAW files (included / add-on)** · prints/extra copies
- Pricing: base + explicit overtime rate + travel/stay; **no hidden album/RAW/copy charges**
- Add-ons: drone · live streaming (NRI kin) · same-day edit/teaser · photo booth · extra albums

### Signature features (differentiators)
- **⏱️ Escrow-backed on-time delivery guarantee** — final payment held until the gallery/album is delivered by the promised date; **delay penalty**; in-app **delivery tracker**
- **🔒 Named-shooter lock** — the specific photographer/team is locked in the contract (automatic for solo; assigned & locked for studios) — kills bait-and-switch
- **🧾 All-in transparent deliverables** — album, RAW, drone, travel all itemized upfront
- **🔐 Privacy/consent toggle** — couple controls whether the studio may showcase their wedding publicly
- **💾 Backup-shooter + dual-card/cloud-backup guarantee** — no lost-footage nightmares

### Supply onboarding (studio or individual, self-serve)
Portfolio (albums/films/reels) · **style tags** (candid / cinematic / traditional / documentary) · offerings (photo / photo+video) · packages & deliverables · **team members each with own portfolio** · **date-wise team availability** (a team shoots one wedding/day) · cities · pricing · RAW/album/drone policy · reviews. **Full sample gallery** required (matching coverage level), verified.

### Process
Delivery-date SLA in contract · final payment released on delivery (escrow) · album revision rounds · usage-rights consent captured.
